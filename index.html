About

Tessa is virtual intelligent assistant created to help assist you Mental Health needs.

Powered with NLU to seem more human and personal, our chatbot analyzes your mood to provide and assist the user. We are also equipped with an easy to use chat function to chat with doctors as well as other patients and friends. To help the patient, we also include many actvities which they can watch and perform from Yoga and CBT Techniques to Mindfullness and Motivational Videos. We also enrich our users with a daily does of happiness articles,to help assist and encourage them. Finally, we have a list of emergency hotlines as well as a method to contact them, with a click of a button.

We hope you enjoy using TESSA Mental Health Services.
